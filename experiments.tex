\section{Experiments}

\noindent\textbf{Dataset}.
We perform our tests on the Dota dataset \cite{9712446}.
The dataset contains 60467700 videos taken from YouTube channels, with a resolution of $1024 \times 720$, annotated with information about the start and end of the anomaly, the category (10 in total) and the bboxes which indicate which objects or persons are involved.
The videos were recorded in different countries and with different light and weather conditions.
The dataset is split in $70\%$ training and $30\%$ validation.
